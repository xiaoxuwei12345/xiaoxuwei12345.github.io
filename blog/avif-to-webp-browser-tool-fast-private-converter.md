---

## Why Convert AVIF to WebP?

Although AVIF offers excellent compression, WebP remains more practical in many real-world use cases due to its broader support.

WebP provides:

- Wide compatibility across browsers and software  
- Good balance between quality and file size  
- Support for transparency  
- Reliable performance in web workflows  

This makes it a safe and practical format for sharing and production use.

## Problems With Traditional Conversion Tools

Most online converters rely on uploading files to remote servers:

- Upload images first

This workflow has several disadvantages:

- Slow due to upload and download time  
- Privacy concerns (files leave your device)  
- File size limits  
- Dependence on third-party services  

For users handling multiple images, this becomes inefficient and inconvenient.
