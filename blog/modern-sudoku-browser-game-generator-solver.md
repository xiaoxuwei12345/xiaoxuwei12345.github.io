# Modern Sudoku in Your Browser – Generator, Solver, and Interactive Puzzle Experience

Sudoku has long been one of the most popular logic puzzles in the world. Its appeal comes from its simple rules combined with deep logical complexity. Each puzzle challenges players to think carefully, eliminate possibilities, and find the correct placement of numbers.

In recent years, Sudoku has evolved beyond paper puzzles and mobile apps into fully interactive browser-based experiences.

---

## 🧩 Why Sudoku Remains So Popular

Sudoku is built on a simple idea:

- A 9×9 grid  
- Numbers 1–9 must appear once per row  
- Numbers 1–9 must appear once per column  
- Numbers 1–9 must appear once per 3×3 block  

Despite these simple rules, solving a Sudoku puzzle requires logical reasoning, pattern recognition, and patience.

---

## 🧠 More Than Just a Puzzle

Modern Sudoku platforms are no longer just static puzzle boards. They now include features that enhance both learning and gameplay:

- Real-time input validation  
- Conflict highlighting  
- Candidate notes (pencil marks)  
- Undo and redo functionality  
- Timers for tracking performance  
- Hint systems for difficult situations  

These features transform Sudoku into a more interactive and educational experience.
