## 🚧 The Problem with Traditional Image Converters

Most online image converters work in a similar way:

- You upload your images to a server  
- The server processes the conversion  
- You download the converted files  

While this works, it introduces several limitations:

- Upload and download delays  
- Privacy concerns (files leave your device)  
- File size restrictions  
- Dependency on external servers  

For users working with sensitive or large image batches, this approach is not always ideal.
