---

## 🎮 Why Minesweeper Is Still So Popular

The rules are simple:

- Click tiles to reveal hidden cells  
- Numbers indicate how many mines are nearby  
- Use logic to avoid hidden mines  
- Clear the board without triggering a mine  

However, beneath this simplicity lies a surprisingly complex puzzle that rewards careful thinking and pattern recognition.

---

## 🧠 A Game of Logic and Strategy

Unlike many modern games that rely on reflexes or fast reactions, Minesweeper is entirely based on reasoning.

Players must:

- Analyze number patterns  
- Deduce safe positions  
- Avoid random guessing when possible  
- Plan moves carefully to minimize risk  

This makes it a timeless puzzle game that continues to challenge new and experienced players alike.
