---

## A Classic Puzzle That Never Gets Old

The core idea of Minesweeper is simple but highly engaging:

- A grid contains hidden mines  
- Each revealed tile shows a number indicating nearby mines  
- The player must logically deduce safe tiles  
- The game ends when all safe tiles are revealed or a mine is triggered  

Despite its simplicity, Minesweeper remains deeply strategic and mentally stimulating.
