---

## 🖼️ Why Convert WebP to PNG?

PNG remains one of the most reliable image formats because it offers:

- Lossless compression  
- Wide compatibility across devices and software  
- Support for transparency  
- Stable behavior in design tools and workflows  

While WebP is efficient for the web, PNG is still preferred for editing and compatibility purposes.

## 🚧 Limitations of Traditional Conversion Tools

Most online converters rely on server-side processing:

This approach has several drawbacks:

- Slow due to upload and download time  
- Privacy concerns (files leave your device)  
- File size limits  
- Dependence on external services  

For users handling sensitive images or large batches, this workflow is inefficient.
